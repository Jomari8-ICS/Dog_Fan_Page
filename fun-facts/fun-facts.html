Things You Might Already Know!

Chihuahuas Stay Small!

It only takes chihuahuas to reach adulthood in the span of 9-12 months! During this period they will stop their growth period. This small dog only reaches a height of 6-9 inches!

Chihuahuas Have Long Lifespans

Chihuahuas, compared to other small dogs, have longer lifespans! The average Chihuahua lifespan is about 12-20 years old!

Things You Might Not Know!

Chihuahuas Have Specific Names For Their Body Parts

There are specific words used for the hair on those long-hair Chihuahuas: on the tips of the ears is called “fringe,” on their legs it is known as “furnishings,” and the fluff on their tail is called the “plume.”

Predictable Colors

Chihuahuas come in seven colors: Black, Black & Tan, Blue & Tan, Chocolate, Chocolate & Tan, Cream, Fawn, Fawn & White, and Red. Purebred Chis also can have one of six standard!

Chihuahuas Are Prone To Phobias

Separation anxiety and fear of traffic or crowds. Due to genetic tendency, these traits are passed down through the generation. But in some dog breeds like the Chihuahua, they are just naturally anxious!
