Chihuahuas Stay Small!

It only takes chihuahuas to reach adulthood in the span of 9-12 months! During this period they will stop their growth period. This small dog only reaches to a height of 6-9 inches!

Chihuahuas Have Long Lifespans

Chihuahuas, compared to other small dogs have longer life spands! The average Chihuahua life span is about 12-20 years old!

Chihuahuas are prone to phobias

Separation anxiety and fear of traffic or crowds.

Chihuahuas have specific names for their body parts

Oddly enough, there are specific words used for the hair on those long-hair Chihuahuas: on the tips of the ears is called “fringe,” on their legs it is known as “furnishings,” and the fluff on their tail is called the “plume.”
